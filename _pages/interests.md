### Park Run
A free, timed 5km fun-run starting every morning at 9am all around the world. Find your nearest Park Run [here](https://www.parkrun.com/countries/) and view my Park Run stats [here](https://www.parkrun.org.uk/parkrunner/1277829/)

### Zoological Society of London (ZSL)
ZSL is the world's oldest wildlife conservation charity and are active in over 700 countries, as well as running London and Whipsnade Zoo. Read about some of ZSL's work [here](https://www.zsl.org/what-we-do) and consider becoming a Fellow [here](https://www.zsl.org/support-us/fellowship).
