### Park Run
Park Run is a free, timed 5km fun-run taking place every Saturday at 9am at local parks across the world. Find your nearest Park Run [here](https://www.parkrun.com/countries/) and view my Park Run stats [here](https://www.parkrun.org.uk/parkrunner/1277829/).

### Zoological Society of London (ZSL)
ZSL is the world's oldest wildlife conservation charity and it conducts scientific operatios in over 70 countries, as well as running London and Whipsnade Zoos. Read about some of ZSL's work [here](https://www.zsl.org/what-we-do) and consider becoming a Fellow [here](https://www.zsl.org/support-us/fellowship). 

### Warwick in Africa (WiA)
Warwick in Africa is an educational charity transforming the teaching of Maths and English in African township schools. Since its founding at the University of Warwick in 2006, WiA has benefited over 750,000 young African learners and trained 5,000 teachers. Read more about Warwick in Africa [here](https://warwick.ac.uk/giving/projects/wia/).
